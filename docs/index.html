SenseHire orchestrates the entire hiring lifecycle, automating the heavy lifting while keeping your team in control.

1

Create a requisition and generate an inclusive job description with structured requirements in minutes.

2

Questions, answered

Frequently asked questions

What exactly is SenseHire?
SenseHire is an enterprise AI hiring platform that unifies sourcing, CV analysis, smart job descriptions, automated AI interviews, candidate outreach, and analytics — replacing a patchwork of recruiting tools with one secure, intelligent system.
How are AI interviews fair and unbiased?
Every candidate answers structured questions scored against the same competency rubrics, with explainable, evidence-backed results. Consistency plus auditability is what makes the process fairer than ad-hoc human screening.
How long does it take to get started?
Most teams are live within weeks. Guided onboarding, prebuilt interview templates, and a question bank get you producing results quickly, and we validate your ROI during a structured pilot.
Is SenseHire secure enough for enterprise?
Yes. SenseHire provides SSO/OIDC identity, role-based access control, multi-tenant isolation, and complete audit trails, with privacy and governance designed in. See our Security & Trust page for details.
