Capabilities

Six core capabilities. Two kinds of value.

Every feature is judged on what it does for the business and for the people who use it every day.

Automated AI Interviews

Conversational, structured interviews powered by AI avatars — with live captions, proctoring, and scoring that adapts to each candidate's role, CV, and competencies.

For the business

Interview every qualified candidate 24/7 without adding headcount — and shrink time-to-hire dramatically.

For the user

Recruiters skip scheduling chaos; candidates interview whenever suits them, on any device.

Sourcing & Talent Hub

Connect talent sources, parse and analyze CVs, and search a unified talent pool with semantic matching that ranks the best-fit people for every requisition.

For the business

Build a reusable talent asset and cut sourcing spend by surfacing candidates you already have.

For the user

Recruiters find qualified people in seconds instead of scrolling endless inboxes.

Smart Job Descriptions

Generate inclusive, on-brand job descriptions and structured requirements in minutes — aligned to competencies and the interview plan that follows.

For the business

Standardize quality and inclusivity across every opening, and post faster.

For the user

Managers go from blank page to publish-ready JD without wrestling with wording.

Talent Drives

Run campus and high-volume hiring at scale — schedule hundreds of interviews in staggered waves with automated logistics and live capacity control.

For the business

Hire entire cohorts in days, not months — perfect for campus and seasonal surges.

For the user

Coordinators run massive events from one screen instead of a wall of spreadsheets.

Analytics & AI Assistant

Role-based dashboards, an AI Analytics Assistant, and enterprise reporting turn hiring data into decisions — on pipeline, velocity, quality, and cost.

For the business

Give leadership real-time, defensible visibility and forecast hiring with confidence.

For the user

Ask plain-language questions and get instant charts — no BI team required.

Outreach & Engagement

Personalized, multi-step outreach campaigns and reusable templates keep candidates informed, engaged, and moving through your pipeline.

For the business

Improve response and offer-acceptance rates with timely, on-brand communication.

For the user

Recruiters launch nurture sequences in clicks; candidates always know what's next.

Templates & Question Bank

Consistent, governed evaluations across teams.

Scheduling & Calendars

Automated booking with slot-capacity control.

Candidate Portal

Prep, system checks, and a smooth interview.

Audit & Governance

Complete trails and role-based control.
