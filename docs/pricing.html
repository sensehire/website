FAQ

Answers for every stakeholder

Choose the lens that fits you — commercial, technical, end-user, or security.

What ROI can we expect from SenseHire?
Customers typically see dramatically shorter time-to-hire, far less manual screening, lower cost-per-hire from tool consolidation, and higher quality of hire. We validate your specific business case during a structured pilot.
How is SenseHire priced?
Plans scale with your team size and hiring volume. Growing teams start on a fixed monthly plan; enterprises get custom pricing aligned to seats, volume, and integrations. Annual billing saves 20%.
How quickly can we go live?
Most teams are live within weeks. Guided onboarding, prebuilt templates, and a question bank get you to measurable results fast.
Does SenseHire replace our ATS?
SenseHire can serve as your end-to-end hiring platform or complement an existing ATS through integrations — many teams consolidate multiple point tools into SenseHire over time.
What is the architecture?
SenseHire is a cloud-native, service-based platform behind a unified API gateway, designed for high concurrency, resilience, and global deployment.
Do you offer APIs and integrations?
Yes. Open APIs and prebuilt connectors integrate SenseHire with your HRIS, calendars, identity provider, and talent sources. See the integrations section.
How does the AI interview engine work?
Interviews are structured and context-aware — follow-ups adapt using the job description, requirements, competencies, and the candidate's CV. Results map to rubrics with explainable, auditable evidence.
Can we configure roles and permissions?
Absolutely. SenseHire ships with eight role types and granular role-based access control so each user sees only what they should.
Is it easy for recruiters to use?
Yes — SenseHire is designed for consumer-grade simplicity. Recruiters get a single workspace for requisitions, candidates, interviews, and outreach.
What is the candidate experience like?
Candidates interview on their own schedule, on any device, with built-in preparation and a system check. Everyone answers the same structured questions for a fair, level playing field.
Do candidates need to install anything?
No. SenseHire runs in the browser, with a quick system check to confirm camera, microphone, and connection before the interview begins.
How do managers review results?
Managers see structured scorecards, transcripts, and analytics, then collaborate and extend offers — all with a complete audit trail.
How is our data protected?
SenseHire is built with security and privacy at its core — SSO/OIDC identity, role-based access control, multi-tenant isolation, and complete audit trails. See the Security & Trust page.
Do you support single sign-on?
Yes. SenseHire integrates with enterprise identity providers via OIDC for single sign-on and centralized access management.
Is everything auditable?
Yes. Key actions are captured in audit trails so you can demonstrate fair, compliant, and well-governed hiring to any stakeholder.
How do you keep AI hiring fair?
Every candidate receives the same structured questions and rubrics, with explainable, evidence-backed scoring — consistency plus auditability is what makes the process more defensible than ad-hoc screening.
